Mudae Note Manager

Quick Start: Paste your $mmasi- output below and click Parse Input.

Command: Use $mmsaty+ri-c+x+kt to include extra info.

Select Characters: Click cards to toggle selection (grayed = deselected).

Add Notes: Click the note area or use the group input to apply notes in bulk.

Sorting: Use the sort buttons in each group (clicking on sort button again causes the ordering numbers to be recalculated)

Ranking: Click the # badge to set your custom order.

Generate: Click Generate buttons to create your $n commands with your selected characters.

Quick Notes: Use the sidebar to store frequently used emojis, notes, and colors for easy reuse.
